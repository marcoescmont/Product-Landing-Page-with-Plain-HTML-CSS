The purpose of bonsai are primarily contemplation for the viewer, ad the pleasant excercise of effort and ingenuity for the grower.

By contrast with other plant cultivation practices, bonsai is not intended for production of food or for medicine. Instead, bonsai pradctice focuses on long-term cultivation and shaping of one or more small trees growing in a container.
